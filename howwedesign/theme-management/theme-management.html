What the Theme Management Committee does

The Theme Management Committee is held monthly to support the Assistant Deputy Minister Web Management Committee by:

  • exploring theme governance opportunities and challenges
  • examining overlaps or gaps in the topic structure that could impact task findability
  • putting forward recommendations that entail significant changes to a theme and/or topic structure
  • sharing ways that support the efficient development of topic structures, topic page links and their descriptions

Learn more about the Theme Management Committee (links to Government of Canada internal website).
