Design system exemptions

Requests for exemptions to elements of Canada.ca design system must go through a review process. Departments have a review board that considers the request for exemption and determines if there are legitimate reasons to proceed. This departmental board can then recommend the exemption to the Enterprise Architecture Review Board (EARB) at Treasury Board Secretariat.

The EARB makes the final decision as to whether there is a legitimate case for an exemption to specific elements of the design system.
