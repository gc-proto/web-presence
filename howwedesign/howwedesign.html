How we design for Canada.ca

Canada.ca is the Government of Canada's unified web presence. Regardless of the technological platform and domain name, it enables user-centric design and consistent experiences for Canadians. To accomplish this, Canada.ca uses a design system: a common look, common templates and patterns, and controlled vocabularies.
