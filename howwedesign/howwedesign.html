How to design for Canada.ca

Canada.ca uses a user-centric, task-based design system to help Canadians find and complete the tasks that matter to them.

Design system

Canada.ca uses a design system: a common look, common templates and patterns, and controlled vocabularies.

  • Canada.ca Content and Information Architecture Specification: information architecture models, topic tree, templates and design patterns
  • Canada.ca Content Style Guide: writing principles, plain language, tone, style, images and video, links, web content makeovers
  • Working templates on GitHub: working examples of the templates, with code
