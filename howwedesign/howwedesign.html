How we design for Canada.ca

Canada.ca is the Government of Canada's unified web presence. The goal is a consistent user experience that helps users quickly accomplish the task they want to complete. To achieve this, Canada.ca uses a design system: a common look, common templates and patterns, and controlled vocabularies.
