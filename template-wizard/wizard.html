You should apply for "Family caregiver benefits." Make sure the medical doctor or nurse practitioner can certify that there was a "sudden change from baseline health."

If their condition deteriorates and the medical doctor or nurse practitioner will certify they are reaching end of life within 26 weeks (six months), the benefit period can be extended with "Compassionate care benefits."
