Find the right template and design patterns for your web content

Answer the following questions to find out if there is a template you should use.

What type of content do you want to publish?

  • Information about programs and services
  • Corporate content: information related to the organization or the minister
  • Navigation: ways to help users navigate to other pages
  • Promotional content
  • Consultation with Canadians

What type of information about programs and services do you want to publish?

  • Launch a service: applying for a program, a benefit, etc.
  • Give background information about a program
  • Make a list of similar elements or an index (documents, publications, etc.)
  • Give information about an Act or Regulation
  • Promote a program or service on another page
  • Add contact information on a page
  • Add a visual element on a page (chart, table, image, video, etc.)
  • None of the above

What type of list of elements do you want to publish?

  • Short list (10 to 50 items)
  • Long list (100+ items)
  • Long list that needs to be filtered
  • Long list with faceted filtering
  • GC-wide collection
  • List of guidance on legislation

Do you want to give information about an Act or a Regulation? 

  • Act
  • Regulation

What type of information about your organization do you want to publish?

  • Official institutional presence (home page for your organization)
  • Contact information for your organization
  • Information about the Minister
  • Report on performance on service standards
  • None of the above

Are you a government organization, institution, or a partnering/collaborative arrangement?

  • A partnering/collaborative arrangement
  • An institution listed under Schedule I, I.1 or II of the Financial Administration Act
  • An organization associated with a federal institution
  • I don't know

What kind of navigation page do you want to publish?

  • Topic page, part of the global topic tree
  • Make a list of several elements or an index (like a list of publications or documents)
  • Link to other related pages
  • Navigate to differrent parts of the page
  • None of the above

What do you want to promote?

  • An initiative or program, through a promotional campaign
  • An event
  • Promote a program or service on an existing page

What do you want to do about consultations? 

  • Launch a new consultation
  • Publish a What we heard report
  • Promote the consultation or the results of a consultation
