    Institutions are required to use this design manual and the style guide for their online content. You can read about the requirement in the Directive on the Management of Communications.

    The elements of this design manual are regularly updated based on the results of ongoing usability testing cycles. They have been developed in collaboration with Government of Canada Theme lead departments.
